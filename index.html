APN Configuration Guide

How to Configure APN

  1. Go to Settings on your phone
  2. Tap Mobile Network or Wireless & Networks
  3. Select Access Point Names (APN)
  4. Tap the + icon to add new APN
  5. Enter the settings shown above
  6. Save and select the new APN
  7. Restart your phone
